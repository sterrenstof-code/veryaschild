ABOUT

Johanna Schauerte and Edna Kleine-Birckenhauer, founders of Veryas Child

Essential and effortless; everything you need in the highest quality, and nothing you don´t.

We started Veryas Child in 2020 because we couldn´t find the skincare we wanted- a pared-back roster of select staples, luxurious in their inherent minimalism. Effective natural products created from the highest quality ingredients and meticulously formulated for daily use.

Started with our now signature Face Oil, we combined our knowledge of cosmetics and chemics to construct an expertly balanced blend of 22 active botanical ingredients that at once cultivate luminous skin, deliver multi-correctional results, and deeply nourish all skin types.

In the last five years we´ve maintained the same strict standards for each material we source and any product we introducte to the collection: We use certified organic and ethically wildcrafted ingredients whenever possible, taking care to partner directly with mindfull manufacturers around the world. Every product is multi-tasking and multi-use, designed for versatility and seamless layering. Our herbal infusions are crafted in-house and we hand blend each formula in small batches to maintain freshness and efficacy. We prioritize eco-friendly. responsible sourced packaging materials and are committed to minimizing waste and footprint.

The line remains a tight edit of higly selective, holistically timeless, effortless essentials. All you need, for any time.
